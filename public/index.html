About This Site

Jun 9th, 2014

Well, it has been a bit longer since my last post than I had hoped. But better late than never, I suppose!

I thought a nice start to these blogs would be a very brief explanation of this website. One of the difficulties for me was dealing with the complications of hosting my own website. It’s not entirely difficult to run a LAMP stack on my Raspberry Pi with a dynamic and free hostname from no-ip, but then there are other issues of speed and security that need to be addressed. Granted, these are not difficult things to learn about with a bit of Googling, and I have learned a lot, but ultimately I just wanted a platform where I didn’t have to constantly mess around with the HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and could focus on the content.

Enter GitHub Pages and Octopress. I won’t bore you with the technical details of these two items as you can read about them on the respective websites, but what I will tell you is that Octopress has bridged a huge gap for me by letting me add content from the command line and edit it using a beautiful and powerful markup language known as Markdown. Markdown allows me to create very nice and formatted posts without the clumsiness of a What You See Is What You Get editor. Now I don’t have a problem with a nice GUI, as my career has me programming and designing one often, but it is refreshing for me to be able to create beautiful content from my clean and simple command line interface.

In case you haven’t noticed, I’ve peppered this article with links. This will be my usual style as it is usually unnecessary and repetitive to copy what someone else wrote. The point of this website is not to make an archive of the internet. But what I hope to do is take whatever skill and expertise that I have to be able to pull information from all over the World Wide Web and tie it together for your edification and interest. Obviously if you ever have any questions about the content you see here, please leave me a comment and I’ll do my best to respond. Thanks for reading!
